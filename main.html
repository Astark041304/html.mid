Sports Car

A sports car is a low, small, usually two-passenger
automobile designed for quick response, easy maneuverability,
and high-speed driving. It is typically a two-seat,
sometimes a four-seat, vehicle characterized by its
nimble abilities, speed, and agility. Sports cars are designed
to deliver a thrilling driving experience, with a focus
on performance, handling, and style.
